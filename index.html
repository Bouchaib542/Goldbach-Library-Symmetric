Independent Researcher in Mathematics, Nantes, France

Introduction

This article explores the symmetric structure of Goldbach pairs for extremely large even numbers. For a given even E, we investigate whether one can always find two primes (p, q) such that p + q = E and p â‰ˆ q. This symmetric formulation is not only aesthetically elegant but also computationally efficient for prediction and verification purposes.

Method

We used a hybrid combination of prime libraries, GPS-based models, and predictive heuristics to generate and verify (p, q) pairs. Each p and q is strictly verified for primality using deterministic algorithms.
